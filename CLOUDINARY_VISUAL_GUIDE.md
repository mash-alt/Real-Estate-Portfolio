## ðŸŽ¬ Animation Flow

1. **Click Upload Button**
   - Widget slides in from center
   - Overlay appears behind

2. **Drag Image Over Drop Zone**
   - Border becomes dashed
   - Background highlights

3. **Upload Progress**
   - Progress bar fills
   - Percentage updates
   - Checkmark on complete

   - Fade-in animation
   - Grid rearranges
   - Counter updates

5. **Hover Image**
   - Lifts up 2px
   - Shadow appears
   - Remove button fades in

   - Fade-out animation
   - Grid reflows
   - Counter decreases
